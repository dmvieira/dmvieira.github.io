About

Senior Staff AI Engineer at Stone, building applied Generative AI systems that deliver real value to clients. My work sits at the intersection of cutting-edge AI research and large-scale product engineering — designing systems that don't just work in a notebook, but ship and scale.

Academic background

PhD from PUC-Rio and Master's in Artificial Intelligence from UFRJ. My research has been cited at top venues including RecSys. I've always believed that rigorous academic foundations and practical product thinking reinforce each other — and I've built my career around proving that.

Entrepreneurship

I've built and accelerated three startups — one of them incubated at the Google Campus in London. Each one taught me something the previous hadn't, and together they've shaped how I think about building products under real constraints.

Community & open source

I'm a frequent conference speaker and a dedicated open-source advocate. I genuinely believe that programming languages are just a medium — a way to help other people. Collaboration is usually the right answer. When it comes to code, sharing is how we all get better.

Outside of work

I love kids, dogs, sports, cold beer with friends, and my family. I do everything for them. You have to take care of your own house before you can help anyone else — and I try to.

Whether I'm designing AI systems, building companies, or on a stage at a conference, the goal is the same: use technology as a catalyst for education and high-impact problem-solving.
